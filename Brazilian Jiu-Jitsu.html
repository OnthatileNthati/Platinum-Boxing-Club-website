Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu

Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu (BJJ) is a martial art and combat sport that focuses on grappling and ground fighting. It emphasizes the use of technique and leverage to control and submit opponents, making it effective for self-defense and competitive fighting. BJJ practitioners learn various submissions, positional controls, and escapes, often training in a gi (traditional uniform) or no-gi attire.
