What Is Muay Thai?

Muay Thai, known as the "Art of Eight Limbs", is a striking martial art that uses fists, elbows, knees, and shins to build strength, endurance, and precision. Our Muay Thai program focuses on proper technique, conditioning, and mental toughness, helping students develop confidence and resilience both inside and outside the gym.

Whether you are a beginner or an experienced fighter, our classes provide a safe, structured, and motivating environment where everyone can progress at their own pace.

Class Structure

  • Warm-Up & Mobility
  • Technique & Fundamentals
  • Pad Work & Bag Drills
  • Partner Drills / Controlled Sparring
  • Cool-Down & Stretching

Who Can Join

  • Beginners (No experience needed)
  • Intermediate practitioners
  • Advanced competitors
  • Men & Women
  • Teens
  • Anyone wanting a full-body workout that's fun and challenging

What You Will Need

  • Gloves
  • Hand Wraps
  • Shin Guards
  • Mouth Guard
  • Comfortable Sportswear

Optional Gear

  • Head Guard
  • Groin Guard
  • Muay Thai Shorts
