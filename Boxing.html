Boxing

Boxing training at Platinum Boxing Club focuses on building strength, endurance, and technique, and discipline through structured, high-quality coaching. Whether you are a beginner or an experienced fighter, our boxing program is designed to improve technique, footwork, conditioning, and mental toughness. Training sessions challenge you physically while helping you develop confidence, focus, and resillience both inside and outside the ring.

  • Water Bottle (Water fountains are available)
  • Boxing Gloves
  • Hand Wraps
  • Water bottle
  • Boxing shoes or Trainers
  • Comfortable Training Clothing

  • Protective gear: mouthguard
  • Positive attitude
