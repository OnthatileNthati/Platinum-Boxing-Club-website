What Is Boxing?

Boxing training at Platinum Boxing Club focuses on building strength, endurance, technique and discipline through structured, high-quality coaching. Whether you are a beginner or an experienced fighter, our boxing program is designed to improve technique, footwork, conditioning, and mental toughness.

Class Structure

  • Warm-up & Mobility
  • Technique Breakdown
  • Conditioning & Drills
  • Optional Sparring
  • Cool down & Stretching

Who Can Join

  • Beginners (No experience needed)
  • Intermediate Boxers
  • Advanced Boxers
  • Men & Women
  • Teens

What You Will Need

  • Boxing Gloves
  • Hand Wraps
  • Boxing shoes or Trainers
  • Comfortable Training Clothing
  • Water Bottle (fountains available)
  • Towel
  • Mouthguard
  • Positive attitude
